# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Home buyers, property investors, and clients comparing premium residential and investment listings for Nqothaka Group Real Estate.

## Product Purpose

Present Nqothaka Group Real Estate as a polished, trustworthy property company and guide visitors from first impression to property search, services, featured homes, testimonials, FAQs, and contact.

## Brand Personality

Premium, calm, architectural. The site should feel refined, spacious, and property-led, with imagery doing most of the persuasive work.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS gradients, purple or blue AI effects, glassmorphism, heavy shadows, overly colorful sections, random decorative blobs, and template-looking card grids that do not match the supplied EverGreen-style references.

## Design Principles

- Match the supplied real estate references before inventing new styling.
- Let large property photography and white space carry the premium tone.
- Keep controls familiar: pill buttons, soft inputs, thin borders, and clear labels.
- Preserve readable contrast on image overlays and sticky navigation.
- Make the mobile experience stacked, calm, and free of horizontal overflow.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls, visible focus states, reduced-motion support, semantic sections, useful alt text for meaningful imagery, and responsive layouts that remain readable on small screens.
